Message Exchanges

What is a Message Exchange?

This is the name we have given to the series of messages between a student and ProPair Advisor that begins with the student’s initial career questions on a particular area.

What’s the different between a public and private Message Exchange?

A public Message Exchange means the Message Exchange is available to all students and schools in the Library. A private Message Exchange is private and therefore not available in the Library.

How does a student book a Message Exchange?

They do this through our platform and follow our career question wizard which helps ensure they submit a high quality first message that gets matched to the best possible Advisor. Please watch this explainer video:

How long does a student have until the Message Exchange closes?

Once a student’s Message Exchange request has been accepted by one of our Advisors and they have received a response to their career questions they have 10 days until the Message Exchange automatically closes. The student can close the Message Exchange before this time.

What if ProPair is unable to find an Advisor for a student’s Message Exchange request?

If we have not found a match for a student after 5 days, then we auto-refund the student’s credits.

Group Analytics

What is Group Analytics used for?

This feature is only available through our Premium Membership. It allows the Educator to visualise the activity of groups i.e. year groups and classes over time.

Membership, Billing & Credits

How do I change my membership plan?

Please submit a request through the platform which you can find in Billings & Credits.

How frequent is the membership fee?

This is paid annually, in advance.

What are Credits?

This is the currency we use on Pr oPair. 1 public Message Exchange costs 1 Credit and 1 private Message Exchange costs 2 Credits.

What are student Credit requests?

This is simply the mechanism by which students can request Credits from their school, parent or third party.

How can I control them?

As an Educator you can decide: 1) whether or not you students are able to request Credits from your school and 2) the maximum Credits a student can request at any one time. These can both be controlled in Settings. Please watch this explainer video:
