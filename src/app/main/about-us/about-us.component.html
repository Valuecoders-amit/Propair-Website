The Problem

Most students are disconnected from the real-life world of work and as a result make sub-optimal decisions about their future.

Schools simply do not have the time & resources necessary to help all their students access career information and advice that's: 1) on demand 2) up to date 3) high quality 4) based on firsthand experience 5) trustworthy 6) personalised and 7) based on a full reflection of the opportunities out there.

This all has negative consequences which can be seen in terms of education and career regret, as well as students not realising their full potential. And it's the disadvantaged students who are impacted most.

How we can help

We help schools bring their careers programme to life by making it easy for their students to access high quality career advice and information from real professionals.

We empower young people to make better-informed decisions about their education & career.

We give busy professionals a flexible, meaningful and quantifiable way to give back and develop themselves in the process.

Our Model & Mision

We set ProPair up as a social enterprise (a community interest company limited by guarantee) so that it would always be true to its mission of unlocking the potential of young people by preparing them for the world of work.

This means ProPair cannot be sold for private gain and all our net profits are reinvested back into the enterprise and social mission (check out our legal constitution here.)

We believe this model is the best way to align the incentives of students, schools, ProPair and our volunteer-base of professionals and create a financially sustainable & high impact enterprise so we can: (i) provide our service free to disadvantaged young people and (ii) achieve our long term goal of donating 100% of our net profits to a selection of charities aligned with our mission.
